The NEB Mist app

Tailor your misting experience.

Devices

Do you want to monitor your habit or reduce your nicotine intake? Perhaps
you want to quit entirely. Wherever you are on your journey with nicotine, NEB
Mist will help you along the way.

Every NEB Mist device has bluetooth connectivity, allowing you to view your
misting routine on an easy-to-use app. The app gives you personalized daily,
weekly and monthly data on usage and nicotine dosages.

One account. Multiple devices support.

All information as Bluetooth connectivity status, serial number and battery level if connected us diplayed on one screen. The color of the device displayed are changing depending on the type of the device owned.

Connect your device

This Interface provides informations regarding a specific device such as
Serial number, Firmware version, Hardware version, Manufacturer name and
UUID. The user can also delete the device from the account.

This will make the device inactive but it will still appear in the device list of the
account. Whenever a software update is available for the device, the
‘Update firmware’ button will appear. Note that the device must be in range
during the entire update process.

Sonics

The Sonics used are grouped by Flavors and Strengths.

Separate flavors have different colors while separate strengths are intensity variation of the color.

Statistics

The app allows the user to consult his consumption statistics. The chart shows the consumption over the last 10 days and multiple filters can be selected to choose between different devices, different sonics or see the overall consumption. The time period can be changed (last 10 days, last month, last year).
